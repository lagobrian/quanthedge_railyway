The problem is annotating the chart patterns and I think I can do it with some help from computer vision techniques.

Besides that, I am working on something about directional dependence. I’ve been interested in this for a while. Like most econometricians, I only know how to model volatility spillovers using DCC-GARCH, Vector Autoregressions, BEKKs, and Diebold-Yilmaz. But the problem with these methods is that while they do a good job of telling you how volatility spills over from one asset to the next or to itself, which is useful, they don’t tell you the direction of the spillovers. Directional dependence does and you can create a network plot of how different assets lead and lag each other. I’m currently working through the math and coding for that.
