If you take 10-15 minutes to read this, I sincerely hope it adds some value to your life, especially your investing life. The only way to make that happen is for me to charge you for it, so I can spend that money doing the best possible research.

It’s really hard to price a newsletter, and I don’t really know what is the ‘correct’ price to charge for it. The only thing I know is that the price has to be enough to reflect the value it brings and to help run me it.
